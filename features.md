## Legal & IP Summary

ISO 20022 is a published financial messaging standard with no IP encumbrances. PCI DSS is a compliance standard maintained by card networks. GDPR/CCPA are legal regulations. OpenID Connect and OAuth 2.0 are open standards. GS1 Digital Link is a published standard. No software patents identified as encumbering loyalty platform core functionality. Gamification research is academic and not proprietary.

## Recommended Feature Scope

**Must-have (MVP)**
- Points-based earning system (purchase-triggered minimum)
- Member account and dashboard
- Basic redemption catalogue
- Tiered membership levels (at least 2-3 tiers)
- Email campaign management
- Integration with major e-commerce platform (Shopify minimum)
- Basic analytics (points earned/redeemed, member count)
- Referral program (basic)
- Member segmentation and targeting
- Rules engine for earning (simple rules)

**Should-have (v1.1)**
- Gamification mechanics (badges, leaderboards, challenges)
- SMS communication and campaigns
- Omnichannel earning (online + in-store)
- Advanced behavioural analytics and segmentation
- A/B testing for program variant testing
- Mobile app for member engagement
- Predictive churn detection
- AI-driven reward recommendations
- Partner/affiliate rewards network
- Real-time personalised offers and nudges

**Nice-to-have (backlog)**
- AI-driven continuous personalisation
- Predictive churn with proactive interventions
- Hyper-personalised gamification per member
- LLM-based customer feedback analysis
- Natural-language campaign authoring
- Dynamic reward pricing
- Coalition programs (multi-brand)
- Blockchain-based transferable points
- Member lifetime value optimisation
- Privacy-first federated learning personalisation
